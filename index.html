Not really a question, but I’ll answer it anyway. Theistic Antitheism is a belief system that promotes praying to and worshipping a god that does not exist.

“Why the fuck would I do that?”

People who live ordinary lives don’t usually come into contact with things that don’t exist. We, on the other hand, love to trip balls on psychedelic drugs. One of the defining features of a psychedelic trip is the perception of things that do not exist. Whilst in a hallucinogenic state, you are capable of being strongly affected by unreal beings such as God. In this unusual state of being, rituals like prayer and worship are quite effective, as they can allow God to reveal Itself to you and guide you in your path.

“So what is God?”

We know It when we feel It.

“Is this some kind of cult?”

Yes. This is a cult. Unlike most cults, however, this one is free to join, has no membership fees, and you can leave whenever you want with no judgments. Seriously, we have no problem with the idea of you wanting to get as far away from this as possible. If you are a normal person, you will not like being in this cult.

“How can I join this cult?”

The most effective way of joining the Church of Theistic Antitheism is to affix a patch bearing the Ellis Sigil to your clothing.
